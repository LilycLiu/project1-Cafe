TERRY'S CAFE

"IF YOU’RE LOOKING FOR A MICHELIN-STAR
RATED RESTAURANT YOU’RE IN THE WRONG
PLACE."

Terry’s Cafe is a diamond in the rough. In an area now saturated with
chains and soulless cheap snacks we’re proud to have achieved
TripAdvisor’s 5-star rating and Certificate of Excellence for two years
running.

TERRY'S DELI

Serving up the best of Terry’s Cafe as
a take away.

The new addition to the Terry’s family serving local produce from Monmouth
Coffee, St. John Bakery and Husk & Honey. We also serve freshly baked
morning goods, home made sausage rolls, award winning sandwiches as
well as vegan & gluten free options - home cooking at its best.

HOME COOKING AT ITS BEST

All our food is cooked on the premises to our family’s recipes. It’s a
thoroughly British affair. If you’re after a traditional English cafe
serving honest, unpretentious, good-value food combined with friendly
service, then look no further.

We’re proud to have achieved TripAdvisor’s 5-star rating and
Certificate of Excellence for two years running. We’re also a Traveller’s
Choice winner again for 2022!

TERRY'S CAFE COOKING KIT

our Terry's cooking kit which includes Terry's Cafe embroidered work
